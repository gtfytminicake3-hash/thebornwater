# Playable Vertical Slice Manual Test

1. Open MainMenuScene
2. Press Play
3. Click NEW GAME
4. Confirm TownScene loaded
5. Check Wood = 100
6. Click COLLECT WOOD
7. Confirm Wood = 110
8. Click BUILD HUT
9. Confirm Wood = 60 and Hut count increased
10. Click ASSIGN WORKER
11. Confirm villager job changed
12. Click ADVANCE TIME
13. Confirm time changed
14. Click SAVE
15. Confirm save log appears
16. Click COLLECT WOOD again
17. Click LOAD
18. Confirm state restored
